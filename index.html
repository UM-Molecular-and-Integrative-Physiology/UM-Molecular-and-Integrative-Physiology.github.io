Why Michigan Physiology?

We provide student-focused PhD training in state of the art physiology research

Our students engage in mentored research training, integrating molecular, cellular and organism approaches, with the goal of understanding human health and disease. Additional individualized training and career development opportunities will prepare you to launch your desired career in science.

  • 01- Top NIH Ranked Physiology Department in the Nation

  • 02- We are Leading Internationally Recognized Research Projects

  • 03- Career Development Opportunities to Meet Your Career Goals

  • 04- Multiple NIH Training Grants to Support Your Research

  • 05- Welcoming, Inclusive and Supportive Environment
